# How to reset your password

<p class="lead">Already have an account for OCCRP Aleph but forgot your password? Learn how to reset your password.</p>

<OCCRPCallout />

<Steps>
  <Step>
    In order to set a password for your new account, go to [aleph.occrp.org](https://aleph.occrp.org) and click on <AnnotationRef>Sign in</AnnotationRef> in the top right corner of the screen.

    <Annotations>
      <Image
        src="/assets/pages/users/getting-started/account/aleph-homepage.png"
        alt="A screenshot of the Aleph homepage. The page header contains a 'Signin' link at the top-right corner of the screen."
      />
      <Annotation
        x="89%"
        y="1%"
        width="9.5%"
        height="5.5%"
      />
    </Annotations>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click on <AnnotationRef>Forgot Password?</AnnotationRef>.

    <Annotations>
      <Image
        src="/assets/pages/users/getting-started/account/keycloak-signin.png"
        alt="A screenshot of the 'Signin' page. The page consists of a form with email and password inputs and a link labeled 'Forgot password?'."
      />
      <Annotation
        x="59%"
        y="55.5%"
        width="12%"
        height="5.5%"
      />
    </Annotations>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Enter your email address and click the **Submit** button.

    <Image
      src="/assets/pages/users/getting-started/account/keycloak-forgot-password.png"
      alt="A screenshot of the 'Forgot password?' page. The page consists of a form with an email input."
    />

  </Step>

  <Step>
    You will receive an email with the subject line **Reset password**. This may take up to 5 minutes. This email contains a link to reset your password.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    You can now set a new password. Choose a unique and long password. We recommend that you use a password manager to generate and store passwords.

    <Image
      src="/assets/pages/users/getting-started/account/keycloak-change-password.png"
      alt="A screenshot of the 'Update password' page. It has two input fields labeled 'New password' and 'Confirm password'."
    />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click the **Submit** button to complete the password reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>
